Characters

Hello Kitty ♡

In the outskirts of London, Hello Kitty was born. Her dearest buddy Mimmy, who is her twin sister, also lives with her parents. She enjoys making new acquaintances and baking cookies. It's true what she often says: "You can never have too many friends!"

Cinnamonroll ♡

The official mascot of Cafe Cinnamon is Cinnamoroll. He occasionally naps on customers' laps and is shy but very nice. By flapping his enormous ears, he can fly through the air.

Kuromi ♡

Kuromi is a tomboy, yet she is actually incredibly girly underneath her rough appearance. She likes reading romance novels and keeping a diary.

My Melody ♡

In Mariland's forest, my Melody was born. Her favorite pastime is helping her mother bake cookies, and she enjoys eating almond pound cake. She adores sharing cake with Flat, a mouse who is her greatest buddy.

Keroppi ♡

On the shore of Donut Pond, the biggest and bluest pond nearby, Keroppi lives with his parents, and siblings in a spacious house. Keroppi's friends like playing games, particularly baseball and boomerangs.
